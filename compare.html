    Compare

     

    This program allows you to compare your monthly electricity consumption in kWh with the average in your state or province.

    - For users in Mexico, enter "MEXICO" instead of a state or province!
    - For users residing in Yukon, Nunavut, Prince Edwards Island or New Brunswick data is not available.
    - Remember to type in all capitals and without spaces or hyphens.
